About dvdrental

Database Design courses require me and my group to create a prototype website. In the end, my group and I agreed to create a website regarding DVD Rental. Then, I individually re-create the design of the website, and also I realized it, although for cart features can not work, because it takes more time. In addition, on this website there is also a watch trailer feature, where users can watch the trailer first before renting a DVD. There's also an about us menu that explains the process we've done so far, as well as what our next goals are. In addition, I also display reviews / testimonials of users who have used our services.
